Board Guidelines
Guidelines:

  • Before creating a post concerning StealthBot, read StealthBot's readme and any and all of the recent announcements, pinned threads, and the Technical Support FAQs. You are also asked to use the board's search feature, in addition to the above, to determine if your question has already been answered. Topics which have already been fully covered will be removed from the forum and may result in further consequences.

  • Spamming is not permitted unless specifically mentioned within the forum's rules. This includes: posting topics in inappropriate forums, posting topics in multiple forums, posting irrelevant or pointless information, unsolicited advertisements, "bumping" threads, double posting, posting excessive caps, large text, or colored text, reposting a trashed and/or deleted thread, etc.

  • Pornography, warez, and any other malicious or illegal activities or transactions may not be discussed or linked to. This includes the discussion, trading or selling of CD-Keys and/or anonymous proxies.

  • There will be no racial, ethnic, or gender based insults or any other personal discriminations. Do not get involved in or respond to flame wars.

  • Users may not dispute or discuss a moderator's action(s) publicly unless given direct permission to do so from either the moderator(s) in question or a member of the administrative staff. Complaints directed at a moderator must first address the moderator in question via personal messaging. If the problem can not be resolved, then the member must send his complaints to a forum administrator. The forum administrator will make or change any and all final decisions.

  • One account per member. If a member is found to have more than one account, that member may have any and/or all of his/her accounts permanently banned from the forum.

Recommended netiquette:

  • When replying to a post, quote the minimum necessary to give some context to your reply and be careful to attribute the quote to the right person.

  • Check the spelling and grammar of your post before submitting it.

  • Before replying to a thread, read the messages that have already been posted there.

  • Use a post title that reflects the content of the message. "Read Me," "Click Here," and other such titles are generally inappropriate and should not be used.

  • If you should find yourself in a disagreement with one person, make your responses to each other via personal messaging rather than continuing your discussion publicly. If you are debating a point on which the forum members might have some interest, you may summarize for them later.

The recommended netiquette is a compilation that was formed by the works of various reputable sources.
